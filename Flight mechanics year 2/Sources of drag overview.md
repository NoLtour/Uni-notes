#### Intro and contents
Recap of drag types:
- [[Drag equation]]
- [[profile drag coefficient]]
- [[induced drag coefficient]]

We will be using $D_{0}$ for zero lift drag (parasitic drag), and $D_{L}$ for lift related drag (induced drag), which directly link to total drag:
$$\begin{align*}
D^{*} &= D_{0} + D_{L}
\end{align*}$$
