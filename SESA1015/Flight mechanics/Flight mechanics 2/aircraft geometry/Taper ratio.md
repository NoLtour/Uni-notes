#### What's an aircraft's
## Taper ratio
This is the ratio between the [[Root chord]] and the [[Mean chord]].

> $$ \lambda = \frac{c_T}{c_R} $$ 
>> where:
>> $\lambda =$ Taper ratio
>> $c_T =$ [[Mean chord]]
>> $c_R =$  [[Root chord]]
