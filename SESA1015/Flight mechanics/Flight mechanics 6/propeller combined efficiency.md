#### What is
## Propeller combined efficiency ($\eta$)
This is the efficiency of the propeller+transmission when converting power into thrust:

> ### $$ P'\eta = P $$ 
>> where:
>> $\eta=$ Efficiency of propeller+transmission
>> $P=$ Power converted to thrust
>> $P'=$ Total power in
