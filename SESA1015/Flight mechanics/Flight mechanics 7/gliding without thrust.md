#### What are the equations for
## Gliding without thrust

![[Pasted image 20211123174004.png]]

$$\begin{align*}
   & & L &= W \cos \theta & D = W\sin\theta\\
\frac{D}{L} &= \frac{W\sin\theta}{W \cos \theta}\\
&= \tan \theta\\
\therefore D&\approx \theta \:\:\:small\:angle\:approx
\end{align*}$$
