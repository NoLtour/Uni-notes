#### How can you model
## Gliding without thrust

$$\begin{align*}
   & & L &= W \cos \theta & D = W\sin\theta\\
\frac{D}{L} &= \frac{W\sin\theta}{W \cos \theta}\\
&= \tan \theta\\
\therefore \frac{D}{L}&\approx \theta \:\:\:small\:angle\:approx
\end{align*}$$
