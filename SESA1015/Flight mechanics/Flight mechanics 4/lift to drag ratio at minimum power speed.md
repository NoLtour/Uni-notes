#### Find the
## Lift to drag ratio at minimum power speed

$$\begin{align*}
& & C_D &= 4 C_{Do} = \frac{4}{3}kC_L^{2}  \\
\therefore \frac{C_L}{C_D} = \frac{C_L}{4C_{Do}} &= \frac{3}{4kC_L  }&&& C_L &= \frac{ \sqrt{ 3 C_{Do} }}{ \sqrt{ k }   }\\
&= \frac{3}{4k   }
\end{align*}$$
