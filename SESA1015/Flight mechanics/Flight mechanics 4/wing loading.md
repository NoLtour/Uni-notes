#### What is
## Wing loading ($w$)
### Definition
This is the weight per unit area on the wing.
> $$ w = \frac{W}{S}$$ 
>> where:
>> $w=$ wing loading
>> $W=$ Weight of plane
>> $S=$ [[Wing plan area]]
