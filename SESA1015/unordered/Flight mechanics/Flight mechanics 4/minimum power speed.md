## Minimum power speed
Power is the rate of doing work, we know that $P=Fv$ so applieing that to drag knowing that drag = thrust:

![[calculating minimum drag#^fb4ad2]]

$$ \begin{align*}
P &= DV \\
&= ( A'V^{2} + \frac{B'}{V^{2}} )V\\
&= A'V^{3} + \frac{B'}{V}\\
\frac{dP}{dV} &= 3A'V^{2} - \frac{B'}{V^2}\\
& & \frac{dP}{dV} &= 0\\
0&=3A'V^{2} - \frac{B'}{V^2}\\
\frac{B'}{V^2}&=3A'V^{2} \\
\frac{B'}{3A'}&=V^{4}\\
\sqrt[4]\frac{B'}{3A'}&=V
\end{align*} $$

So:
> $$ V_{MP}= \sqrt[4]\frac{B'}{3A'} $$
