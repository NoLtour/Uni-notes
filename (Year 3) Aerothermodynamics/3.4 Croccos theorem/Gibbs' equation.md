## Gibbs' equation

> ### $$\begin{align*}\\ \text{intensive: } && G(p,T)  &= U + pV - TS =  H - TS \\\\ \text{specific: }&& G(p,T)  &= u + pv - Ts =  h - Ts  \end{align*}$$ 
> ### $$\begin{align*} Tds &= dh - \frac{dp}{\rho} \end{align*}$$ 
>> where:
>> $G(p,T)=$  [[Gibbs free energy]]
>> $U=$  the [[internal energy]]
>> $p=$  pressure
>> $V=$  volume
>> $T=$  temperature
>> $H=$  [[enthalpy]]
>> $S=$  [[entropy]]
>> $u=$  specific internal energy
>> $v=$  specific volume
>> $h=$  specific enthalpy
