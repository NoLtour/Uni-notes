## Prandtl number

> ### $$\begin{align*} \text{Pr}  &= \frac{c_{p} \mu}{k}  \end{align*}$$
>> where:
>> $\text{Pr}=$ [[Prandtl number]]
>> $c_{p}=$ [[constant pressure specific heat]]
>> $\mu=$ [[viscosity|dynamic viscosity]]
>> $k=$ [[material thermal conductivity|thermal conductivity]]
