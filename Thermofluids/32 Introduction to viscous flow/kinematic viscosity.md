#### What is
## Kinematic viscosity ($\nu$)
This is just the [[viscosity|dynamic viscosity]] per denisty, often used in momentum heave equations.

> ### $$ \nu = \frac{\mu}{\rho} $$ 
>> where:
>> $\nu=$ [[kinematic viscosity]]
>> $\mu=$ [[viscosity|dynamic viscosity]]
>> $\rho=$ density
