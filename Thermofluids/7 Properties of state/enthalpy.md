#### What is
## Enthalpy $E_{h}$
Enthalpy is a property of a thermodynamic [[system (thermodynamics)|system]], is the sum of the system's [[internal energy]] and the product of its pressure and volume.

> ### $$ E_{h} = PV $$ 
>> where:
>> $E_{h}=$ [[enthalpy]]
>> $P=$ Pressure
>> $V=$ Volume
