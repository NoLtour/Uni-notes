# Boolean algebra

> ### $$ \begin{align*} A+1 &= 1\\ A\cdot0 &= 0\\  A \cdot \bar{A} &= 0\\  A + \bar{A} &= 1 \\  AA &= A+A = A \\  \bar{\bar{A}} &= A \\ A(B+C) &= AB+AC\\  A + BC &= (A+B)(A+C)\\  A(A+B) &= A\\ A + \bar{A}B &= A+B\\  \overline{(A+B)} &= \bar{A}\cdot\bar{B}\\  \overline{(AB)} &= \bar{A} + \bar{B}\\ \end{align*} $$
