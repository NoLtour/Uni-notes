#### Whats the method for
## Calculating power of an AC circuit
### RMS
> ### $$ V_{rms} = \frac{V_{P}}{\sqrt{2}} $$ 
>> where:
>> $V_{rms}=$ root mean squared voltage 
>> $V_{P}=$ peak voltage


> ### $$ I_{rms} = \frac{I_{P}}{\sqrt{2}} $$ 
>> where:
>> $I_{rms}=$ root mean squared current 
>> $I_{P}=$ peak current
