#### What is
## Magnetic loading ($B$)
The total flux in the air gap of a machine is called magnetic loading. It is measured in [[tesla]]. This loading sets the limit on working flux without saturation. 

> ### $$ B = \frac{\phi_{m}}{A} $$ 
>> where:
>> $B=$ [[magnetic loading]]
>> $\phi_{m}=$ [[magnetic flux]] in motor
>> $A=$ area
