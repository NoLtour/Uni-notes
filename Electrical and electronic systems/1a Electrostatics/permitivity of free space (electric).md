#### What is the
## permitivity of free space ($\epsilon_0$)

> $\epsilon_0 = 8.854187\cdot 10^{-12}Fm^{-1}$

This is the value of the absolute dielectric permittivity of a classical vaccum, and is measured in farads per meter.
