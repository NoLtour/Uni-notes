#### What is
## Resistance
This is the measure of an objects opposition to the flow of [[Electric current]]. It can be calculated using a materials [[resistivity]]:

> $$ R = \frac{\rho l}{A} $$ 
>> where:
>> $R=$ resistance
>> $\rho=$ [[resistivity]]
>> $l=$ Length
>> $A=$ Cross sectional area
