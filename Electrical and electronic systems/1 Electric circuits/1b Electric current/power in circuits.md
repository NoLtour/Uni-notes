#### How do you calculate
## Power in circuits
We know [[Electric current#^c373aa]] and [[potential difference#^d7f59c]] so:

> $$ W = \frac{E}{t} = \dfrac{VQ}{(\dfrac{Q}{I})} = VI $$ 
>> where:
>> $W=$ power (watts) 
>> $V=$ [[potential difference]]
>> $I=$ [[Electric current]]
