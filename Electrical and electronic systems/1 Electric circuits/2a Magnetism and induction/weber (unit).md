#### What is a
## Weber (unit)
Weber is a unit of [[magnetic flux]].

> $$ \phi = Tm^{2} = \frac{Nm}{A} $$ 
>> where:
>> $\phi=$ [[magnetic flux]] (in weber (Wb)) 
>> $T=$ [[tesla]]
>> $m=$ meter
>> $N=$ Newton
>> $A=$ [[Electric current|ampere]]
