## Phasor manipulation

> ### $$ (a \angle \alpha) \times (b \angle \beta) = ab \angle (\alpha + \beta) $$
> ### $$ \frac{a \angle \alpha}{b \angle \beta} = \frac{a}{b} \angle (\alpha - \beta) $$ 
> ### $$ \frac{1}{a \angle \alpha} = \frac{1}{a} \angle (-\alpha) $$ 
>> where:
>> $a,b=$ [[phasor]] magnitudes
>> $\alpha,\beta=$ phasor angles
