#### How do you find the
## Energy stored in a capacitor
You use:
> $$ E = \frac{1}{2}CV^2 $$ 
>> where:
>> $E=$ work done 
>> $C=$ [[capacitance]]
>> $V=$ potential difference

This gives you the energy stored in the electric field between the plates.
