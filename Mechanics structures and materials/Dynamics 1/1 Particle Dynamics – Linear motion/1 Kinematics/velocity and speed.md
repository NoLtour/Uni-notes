## Velocity and speed
Sometimes people use velocity and speed interchangably, which is wrong. Speed is average velocity:

> ### $$ v_{avg} = \frac{\Delta s}{\Delta t} $$ 
>> where:
>> $v_{avg}=$ average velocity 
>> $\Delta s=$ change in position
>> $\Delta t=$ change in time
