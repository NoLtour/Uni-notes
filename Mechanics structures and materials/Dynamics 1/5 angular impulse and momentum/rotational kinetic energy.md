#### What is
## Rotational kinetic energy
This is the energy stored in the rotation of a body.

> ### $$ KE_{rotational} = \frac{1}{2} I \omega^{2} $$ 
>> where:
>> $KE_{rotational}=$ energy
>> $I=$ [[moment of intertia]]
>> $\omega=$ angular velocity
