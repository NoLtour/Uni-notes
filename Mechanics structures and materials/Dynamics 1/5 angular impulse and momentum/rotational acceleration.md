#### What is the equation for
## Rotational acceleration
> ### $$ \tau = I \alpha $$ 
> ### $$ \tau = Fr $$
> ### $$ \alpha = \frac{Fr}{I} $$
>> where:
>> $\tau=$ torque
>> $F=$ force
>> $r=$ distance from centre of rotation
>> $I=$ [[moment of intertia]]
>> $\alpha=$ angular acceleration
