## Acceleration in the n-t coordinate system
![[Pasted image 20220201173804.png]]

We know that acceleration is equal to rate of change of velocity:
$$\begin{align*}
a = \frac{d\underline{v}}{dt} &   &\underline{v}&= v u_{t}\\
&= \frac{d v u_{t}}{dt}
\end{align*}$$
