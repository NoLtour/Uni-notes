#### What is a
## Impulse
A impulse is basically the change in momentum due to force over some time period:

$$\begin{align*}
F &= ma\\
&= m \frac{dv}{dt}\\
\int^{t_{1}}_{t_{2}} F dt &=\int^{v_{1}}_{v_{2}} m dv\\
&= mv_{2} - mv_{1}\\
&= Impulse
\end{align*}$$
