#### What is
## Ductility
The amount of plastic deformation which can occur before failure.

> $$ Ductility = \%Elongation $$
> $$ or $$
> $$ Ductility = \%Reduction\:in\:cross\:sectional\:area $$

#### Brittleness

We define a material as brittle if it's %elongation is less ant 5%
