### Material combinations
There are a range of combinations:
- Metal/Metal
- Metal/Ceramic
- Metal/Polymer
- Ceramic/Ceramic
- Polymer/Polymer

Basically just about anything, where both materials are somewhat distinct.

### Name convention
The more expensive material is placed first in the name.
