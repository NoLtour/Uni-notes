#### What are the
## Polar second moment of area standard resualts

### Circular cross section

> ### $$ J = \frac{\pi R^{4}}{2} $$ 
>> where:
>> $J=$ [[polar second moment of area]]
>> $R=$ Radius of circle

### Ring cross section

> ### $$ J = \frac{\pi(R_o^{4} - R_i^{4})}{2} = \frac{\pi R_o^{4}(1 - n^{4})}{2} $$ 
>> where:
>> $J=$  [[polar second moment of area]]
>> $R_o=$ outer radius
>> $R_i=$ inner radius
>> $n= \dfrac{R_i}{R_0}$
