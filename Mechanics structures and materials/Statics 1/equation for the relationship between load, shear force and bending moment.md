## Equation for the relationship between load, shear force and bending moment

> $$ \int^{M}_0 dM = M(x) = \int^{x}_0 Q dx $$ 
>> where:
>> $M=$  The moment on the beam
>> $x=$ The position along the beam
>> $Q=$ The load on the beam
