#### What is
## Guage pressure
When pressure is measured relative to atmospheric pressure we call it guage pressure.

> ### $$ P_{G} = P - P_{A}  $$ 
>> where:
>> $P_{G}=$ [[guage pressure]] 
>> $P_{A}=$ atmospheric pressure
>> $P=$ actual pressure
