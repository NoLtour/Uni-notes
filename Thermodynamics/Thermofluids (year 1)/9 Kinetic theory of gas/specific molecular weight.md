## Specific molecular weight ($M,m_{u}$)

This is a constant that describes the weight of a single [[mole]] of some substance:

> ### $$\begin{align*} m &= nM  \end{align*}$$
>> where:
>> $m=$ mass of substance
>> $n=$ number of [[mole]]s for that mass
>> $M=$ [[specific molecular weight]]
