## Potential difference
Potential difference or commonly known as "voltage" is the difference in energy potential per charges between two points, an effective analogy is water pressure but with electrons.

> $$ V = E/Q $$ 
>> where:
>> $V=$ Voltage
>> $E=$ Energy
>> $Q=$ Coulomb
