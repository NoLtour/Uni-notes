#### What's
## shear stress
This is when [[Stress]] isn't purly tensile or compressive, shear stress is when the force acts parallel to a cross-sectional area of a body.

![[Pasted image 20211022204858.png]]

To calculate shear stress you use:

> $$ \tau = \frac{F}{A} $$ 
>> where:
>> $\tau=$ Shear stress 
>> $F=$ Force
>> $A=$ Area
