#### What is the equation for
## Calculating stored strain energy in a crack
> ### $$ U_E = \frac{(\sigma_0)^{2} \pi a^2 }{E} $$ 
>> where:
>> $U_E=$  Stored strain energy
>> $\sigma_0=$ stress across the material
>> $a=$ radius of crack
>> $E=$ [[Youngs modulus]] of material
