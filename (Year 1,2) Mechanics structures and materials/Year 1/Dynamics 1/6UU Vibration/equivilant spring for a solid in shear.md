#### What is the equation for a
## Equivilant spring for a solid in shear

![[Pasted image 20220302101627.png]]

> ### $$ k = \frac{GA}{L} $$ 
>> where:
>> $k=$ [[spring constant]]
>> $G=$ [[shear modulus]]
>> $A=$ unloaded cross sectional area
>> $L=$ unloaded length of solid (shear face to shear face)
