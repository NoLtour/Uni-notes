#### What is a
## Upper-triangular matrix
Its just a matrix where everything below the diaginal is zero:

> ### $$ A = \begin{pmatrix} a & b & c & d \\ 0 & e & f & g \\ 0 & 0 & h & i \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & j \end{pmatrix} $$ 
>> where:
>> $A=$ [[square matrix]]
>> $a,b,c...j=$ are elements of the matrix
