#### What is a
## Partial differential equation
#### Definition
A partial differential equation (PDE) is an equation which imposes relations between the various partial derivatives of a multivariable function. 

#### Example
$$\begin{align*}
   u(x,y,z) &= \sqrt{ x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} } 
\end{align*}$$
