## Electromagnetic
This is a kind of [[electrically propelled systems|electrically propelled system]], which uses the crossing of an electric and magnetic fields to create a [[Lorentz's force law|Lorentz force]] in the gas (plasma). It requires a high current and a heavy propellant such as Xenon but are reasonably compact and offer high ISPs.
