#### What are
## Inconsistant equations
A set of linear equations with no consistant solutions for example:
$$\begin{align*}
2x+7y+3z &= 3\\
2x+9y+3z &= 3\\
y&= 1\\ 
\end{align*}$$
The opposite are [[consistant equations]] which are well... consistant.
