## Derivative of simple exponent
### Problem
How would you derive $y=ax^b$?

### Method
You can use the long method from [[Proof of basic differentiation]], but the faster method is simply:
> $$ y=ax^b \rightarrow \dfrac{dy}{dx} = b*ax^{b-1} $$ 
> > where:
> > $a =$ constant
> > $b \neq$ 0
