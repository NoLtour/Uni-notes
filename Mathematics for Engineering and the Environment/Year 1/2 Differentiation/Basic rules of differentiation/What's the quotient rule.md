## What's the quotient rule
> $$ \dfrac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{u}{v}\right)  = \dfrac{v\dfrac{du}{dx} - u\dfrac{dv}{dx}}{v^2}  $$ 
> $$alternatively$$
> $$ \dfrac{d}{dx}\left(\frac{u}{v}\right) = \dfrac{v\dot{u} - u\dot{v}}{v^2}  $$ 
>> where:
>> $u = f(x)$
>> $v = g(x)$
