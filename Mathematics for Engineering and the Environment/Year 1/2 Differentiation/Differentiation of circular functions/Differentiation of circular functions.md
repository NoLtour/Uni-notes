## Examples

#### Example 1
Differentiate $y=\cos(5x+4)+\tan(x^3+2x^2-x+69)$

$$ 
\begin{align*}
 y=\cos(5x+4)&+\tan(x^3+2x^2-x+69)\\\\
\dot{y}=-5\sin(5x+4) &+ (3x^2+4x-1)\sec^2(x^3+2x^2-x+69)
\end{align*}
$$
