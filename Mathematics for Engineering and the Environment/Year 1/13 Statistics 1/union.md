#### What is a
## Union ($\cap$)

Litterally just an and statement:

> ### $$ A \cap B $$ 
>> where:
>> $A,B=$ some event 
>> $\cap=$ [[union]]

Usually used to express the probability of something happening but can also be used to express logic.
