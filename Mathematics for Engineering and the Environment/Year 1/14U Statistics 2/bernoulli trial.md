#### What is a
## Bernoulli trial
A Bernoulli trial is a single observation of a random variable X, say, that can take the values 0 or 1 (false/true).

Hence:
> ### $$ P(X=0) = 1-P(X=1)  $$ 
> ### $$ 1 = P(X=0)+P(X=1)  $$ 
>> where:
>> $P(X=1):$ probability $X=1$
>> $P(X=0):$ probability $X=0$
