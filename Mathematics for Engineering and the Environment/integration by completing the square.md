#### What is
## Integration by completing the square

### Theory
For some things like $\int \frac{1}{x^{2} + 2x +5} \cdot dx$ you can't solve it conventionally so:

$$\begin{align*}
\int \frac{1}{x^{2} + 2x +5} \cdot dx\\
\int \frac{1}{ (x + 1)^{2} +5} \cdot dx
\end{align*}$$
