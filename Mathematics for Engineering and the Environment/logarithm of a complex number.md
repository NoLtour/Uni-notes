#### How do you find the
## Logarithm of a complex number

Consider the equation $z = e^{w}$ if we express $z=x+iy$ and $w=u+iv$:

$$\begin{align*}
z &= e^{w} \\
 && z&=x+iy & w&=u+iv \\
 x+iy &= e^{u+iv}\\
&= e^{u}e^{iv}\\
&= e^{u} (\cos v + i\sin v)
\end{align*}$$

Hence we can separate into real and imaginary parts:
$$\begin{align*}
&& x &= e^{u} \cos v & y &= e^{u} \sin v\\
&= x^{2} + y^{2}\\
&=(e^{u} \cos v)^{2} + (e^{u} \sin v)^{2}\\
&= e^{2u} ( \cos v  )
\end{align*}$$
