## Solution of linear differential equations

> For an equation of the form:
> ### $$ \frac{dx}{dt} + p(t) \times x = r(t) $$ 
> The solution is:
> ### $$ x = e^{-f(t)} \left[ \int e^{f(t)} \times r(t) \cdot dt + C \right] $$
> ### $$ f(t) = \int p(t) \cdot dt $$
