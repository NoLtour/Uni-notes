## First shift theorem for [[Laplace transform]]

### Theorem

> ## $$ \mathcal{L}[e^{-ax}f(x)] = \tilde{f}(s+a) $$ 
>> where:
>> $a=$ some constant
>> $\mathcal{L}=$ [[Laplace transform]] function
>> $\tilde{f}(s+a)=$ [[Laplace transform]] of $e^{-ax}f(x)$
