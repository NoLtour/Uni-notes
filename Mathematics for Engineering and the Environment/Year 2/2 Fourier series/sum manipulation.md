## Sum manipulation

> ### $$ \sum\limits^{B}_{n=A} f(n) = \sum\limits^{-A}_{n=-B} f(-n) $$ 
>> where:
>> $A,B=$ limits such that $A<B$ 
>> $f(n)=$ some function of $n$


#### Example

$$\begin{align*}
\sum\limits^{5}_{n=1} xn^{2} &= ? & \sum\limits^{-1}_{n=-5} x(-n)^{2} &= ? \\
&= x + 4x + 9x + 16x + 25x & &= 25x + 16x + 9x + 4x + x\\
&= 55x & &= 55x
\end{align*}$$
