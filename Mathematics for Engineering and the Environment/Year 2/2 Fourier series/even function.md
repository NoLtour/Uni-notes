## Even function


> ### $$ f_{e}(x) = f_{e}(-x) $$ 
> ### $$ \int f_{e}(x) \cdot dx = g(x) $$ 
> ### $$ \int^{A}_{-A} f(x) \cdot dx = 2 \int^{A}_{0} f(x) \cdot dx $$
>> where:
>> $f(x)=$ some [[even function]]
>> $g(x)=$ some [[odd function]]
>> $A=$ any value

A function that satisfies $f(x)=f(-x)$. On a chart that looks like being mirrored in the y axis.
