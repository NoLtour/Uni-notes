## Odd function

> ### $$ f(x) = -f(-x) $$ 
> ### $$ \int f(x) \cdot dx = g(x) $$ 
> ### $$ \int^{A}_{-A} f(x) \cdot dx = 0 $$
>> where:
>> $f(x)=$ some [[odd function]]
>> $g(x)=$ some [[even function]]
>> $A=$ any value

A function that satisfies $f(x)=-f(-x)$. On a chart that looks like being rotatable 180 degrees around the origin.
