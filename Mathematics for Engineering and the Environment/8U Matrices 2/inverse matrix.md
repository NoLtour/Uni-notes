#### How can you find an
## Inverse matrix

> ### $$ A^{-1} = \frac{1}{|A|} adj\:A $$ 
>> where:
>> $A^{-1}=$ [[inverse matrix]] 
>> $|A|=$ [[determinant]] of A
>> $adj\:A=$ [[adjoint matrix]]
>> $AA^{-1} = I$
>> $I=$ [[unit matrix]]

If you look back at the [[adjoint matrix]] page you can see under it's strange properties how it can easily be manipulated to give an inverse matrix.
