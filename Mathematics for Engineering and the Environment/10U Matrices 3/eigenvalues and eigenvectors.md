## Eigenvalues and eigenvectors

The eigenvalues of a [[square matrix]] can be found using:
> ### $$ 0 = \det ( \lambda I - A) $$ 
>> where:
>> $\lambda=$ [[eigenvalues and eigenvectors|eigenvalues]] 
>> $I=$ [[unit matrix]]
>> $A=$ some [[square matrix]]
