## Power series

> ### $$ a_{0} + a_{1} x + a_{2} x^{2} + a_{3} x^{3} + .... + a_{n} x^{n} + ... $$ 
>> where:
>> $a_{i}=$ are independent of $x$

So the [[Maclaurin series#^2648d4|Maclaurin series]] can be described as a [[power series]].
