## Proof of differentiating tring inverse functions

To differentiate $\sin^{-1}(x)$

$$ 
\begin{align*}
	y&=\sin^{-1}(x)\\
\sin(y) &= x\\\\
\sin^2(y)&=x^2\\
&\sin^2(y)+\cos^2(y)=1\\
&\sin^2(y)+\cos^2(y)=1
\end{align*}
$$
