#### What is a
## Transposed matrix ($A^{T}$)
Basicall this is where you flip a matrixes rows and columns.

### Example
##### Question
> Given $A$:
> $$ A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3  \\ 4  & 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 & 9 \end{pmatrix} $$
> Find $A^{T}$

##### Answer
> $$ A^{T} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 4 & 7  \\ 2 & 5 & 8 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \end{pmatrix} $$
